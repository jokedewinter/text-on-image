Studious and critical practice

My work explores the relationship between multiculturalism and midlife subcultures.

With influences as diverse as Munch and John Cage, new synergies are generated from both constructed and discovered structures.

Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of the mind. What starts out as triumph soon becomes corrupted into a manifesto of greed, leaving only a sense of decadence and the unlikelihood of a new understanding.

As spatial phenomena become reconfigured through studious and critical practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the limits of our world.
